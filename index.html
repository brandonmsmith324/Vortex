Welcome to Vortex Autosports

Vortex Autosports was founded by i0milesniper and Cobra3658 on September 5, 2018 and is a community of like minded people that want to race clean and socialize together. We have members online racing every night and welcome anyone who wants to join a lobby to race.

What is a Race Team?

Race teams are comprised of racers who are all trying to reach a common goal. They all want to help their team become the best they can be by beating other rival teams. Teammates must work together to help each other be the best drivers they can be.

Teamwork

As the saying goes, “There’s no ‘I’ in team.” A great teammate is willing to play any role on the team. Having a “team-first” attitude with the will to fulfill any role necessary enables the team’s best chance of being successful. A great teammate gives maximum effort. Never give less than your best. As cliché as it sounds, hard work beats talent when talent doesn’t work hard.
