Tips and Tricks

  • Do as much practice as you can without assists. You don't have to turn them all off at once, just gradually start removing them to where you get comfortable driving without them, then start removing some more. Get to the point where you are able to race without assists, you will be much quicker.
  • A loose car is a fast car
  • Be smooth. Jerky movements on the steering wheel and treating the brakes and accelerator like on/oﬀ switches are not the way to go
  • Learn the track, know where your braking points are.
  • Use your teammates for help. Ask if they can see anything you can improve on.
  • Use your Practice time! Go out there and work on areas you're having trouble with. Try different lines or approaches to see if something works better for you.
  • Play around with tunes to see if something else works. Click the banner below to a general Tuning Guide
  • Notify teammates on other drivers who are causing trouble on the track.
  • Don't wreck your teammates!!!
